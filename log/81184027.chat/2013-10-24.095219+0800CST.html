(11:11:09 AM) 光: 信與不信不能同負一軛？How Skeptics and Believers Can Connect 2013-04-12 19:02:47 T. M. Luhrmann, 斯坦福大學人類學教授、 《When God Talks Back: Understanding the American Evangelical Relationship With God》一書作者在紐約時報發表評論《How Skeptics and Believers Can Connect》討論美國近年來宗教信徒和無神論者愈演愈烈的針鋒相對(http://www.nytimes.com/2013/04/07/opinion/sunday/how-skeptics-and-believers-can-connect.html?_r=0)。 正如政治學者Robert D. Putnam和David E. Campbell在《American Grace》一書中所說的 “recent years have seen the sharpest points of disagreement between religious believers — of nearly all stripes — and those who denounce religious belief of all types.”

對於這種棘輪效應式的對立，人類學家有個專門的術語來形 容："schismogenesis": Gregory Bateson developed the word to describe mirroring interactions, where every move by each side makes the other respond more negatively, like those horrible arguments with your spouse where everything you say makes the other person dig in their heels more fiercely. " Luhrmann認為這種虛妄的非此則彼令信徒和非信徒難以互相尊重，“有如在沙上划界，你只能選一邊站隊。”

Luhrmann 以她的田野經歷說明信徒和非信徒其實並非如此迥異。 "When I arrived at one church I had come to study... I saw my own doubts, anxieties and yearnings reflected in those around me. People were willing to utter sentences — like 'I believe in God' — that I was not, but many of those I met spoke openly and comfortably about times of uncertainty, even doubt. Many of my skeptical friends think of themselves as secular, sometimes profoundly so. Yet these secular friends often hover on the edge of faith. They meditate. They keep journals. They go on retreats. They just don’t know what to do with their spiritual yearnings. "

那麼二者是否還有和解、溝通，跳出這種對立的牢籠呢？ "Good marriages work because couples learn to repair, rather than escalate, their conflicts. Same-sex marriage and abortion should not be approached by drawing a line in the sand and demonizing everyone on the other side. We need to recognize something of what we share, and to carry on a conversation — and if we can keep the conversation going, we will, however slowly, move forward. If we can’t, we’re in real trouble. "

這一觀點，不但適用於宗教schismogenesis, 也對政治schismogenesis適用。雖然文章針對的是美國的情況，但對於今天的中國，亦能帶來不少反思。
We are the power.
